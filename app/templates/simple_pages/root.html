The Address Changer

Are you annoyed by changing or even forgetting to change your address with all your accounts?

We got you!

The Address Changer - the best way to keep track of all your accounts and your easy way of changing your address for all your accounts with one click!

Easy to set up

It is super easy to set up an Address Changer account. Just sign up with your email address and a password. After the sign up add your account details with the button "Account Settings".

Easy to add accounts

When you are logged in you can add companies with the "Add Company" button. You can also easily edit and delete companies.

Change your address with one click

When you added all your accounts and you move to a new location you just need to change your address in the account settings. After that you only need to click "Send Emails to all accounts". That's it! Now your address is changed for all your accounts.
